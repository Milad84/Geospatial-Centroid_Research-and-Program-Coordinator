cucurbitaData.csv - occurrence data for three species : g and h are categorical values associate with genebank or herbarium samples 

proAreas.tif - protect lands layer based on https://www.protectedplanet.net/en/thematic-areas/wdpa?tab=WDPA converted to binary raster, 1 = protected lands


cucubitaRasters.rda - raster stack of predicted distribution models for the three species of interest

ecoregions.rda - a shp of WWF ecoregions crop to approximate extent of the modeled area 

example data is from 
https://github.com/CIAT-DAPA/GapAnalysis

original research can be found here
https://nph.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/ppp3.10085
